What is Shoofly Pie?

Shoofly pie is a sweet molasses crumb pie rooted in Pennsylvania Dutch heritage. With variations like wet bottom and dry bottom, this sticky treat has been delighting locals for generations. Whether you prefer the gooey filling of a wet bottom or the more cake‑like texture of a dry bottom, you'll find a slice that suits your taste.
